# SYNAPTIX-SKINLYTICS
An AI-powered app that scans your face and outfit to recommend the perfect makeup shades, steps, and looks tailored to your skin tone and occasion.
# Problem Statement
Consumers struggle to identify products suitable for their skin type and tone.
They often:
Browse multiple websites
Rely on influencer trends
Experiment with unsuitable products
Spend time searching for matching foundations and concealers
This leads to confusion, wasted money, and skin damage.
# Solution
Skinlytics eliminates trial-and-error by providing:
AI-based selfie skin analysis
Skin tone and texture detection
Personalized skincare routines
Foundation and concealer shade recommendations
Direct product suggestions based on individual skin profile
Users can receive accurate recommendations instantly within the app instead of searching across multiple platforms.
